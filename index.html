My dearest, Maʻam Dhara️♡

You are still as bright as I first saw you.
A one of a kind, hard to bid adieu.
To love so pure, purely to ward off woe.
Te amaré hasta morir—Te amo.

I thank the universe for meeting you,
The stars too for experiencing you
Plethora of colors embodied by you
Holding up a fluorescent, ethereal hue.

Gosh, I would kill to re-experience such
Perhaps it is too much to ask for such.
Although I am aware things never last
The light you bring in me will remain vast.

Never did I felt an older personʻs warmth
At the very least, a warmth close to yours.
It baffles me how you hold so much worth
Even so, I will welcome you like the shores.

The shores I believed to be out of reach,
Only to have my feet wet at the beach.
Quite luring you to walk in depths of dreams
Uttering that there are more than just whims.

Graceful & fleeting like the white lilies
Would go as well as for a diamond.
Still one that stands out among all of these—
That Iʻd be the pearl to your rose diamond.

Your pearl whom will silently yearn for you.
Fail to alter your ever-glowing hue.
For I sought you in places you’ve never been.
Insisting that someday you will be seen.

With all the nonsensical folks around
They can be a pain and all except for one,
And that is with hindsight to be more bound
To appreciate how there is you than none. X0

I find my circumstances amusing.
Life really could be unpredictable—
Who wouldve known onto who I am clinging?
That a teacher brings love so palpable?

I will never forget your calm adobe.
It held a significant role in me
And a source of sharing whatʻs yours to me.
I felt trusted by you, that is the role.
